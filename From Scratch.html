This hobby started when I decided to make my mom a ring for christmas.I found some tool wire from the garage and an old pair of pliers and made a woven heart shaped ring for her and she loved it! My dad saw it and said I need to do more with this, so he bought me a whole tool kit that I still use today! I am so grateful for him and the support he and the rest of my family has given to me
